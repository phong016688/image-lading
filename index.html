FAQ

Common release questions.

Does PhotoClean AI delete photos automatically?

No. The app groups and suggests cleanup candidates, while users review and confirm important actions.

Are photos uploaded to a server?

The first release is local-first. Core cleanup flows run on device to reduce privacy risk.

Does it support both iPhone and Android?

Yes. The Flutter app is configured for iOS and Android, and this landing page includes CTAs for both stores.
